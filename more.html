Productivity

I am a bit of a productivity nerd. If there's a productivity app out there, chances are I've tried it. I love many of them (Beeminder, Productivity Owl and Productivity Challenge Timer being my favourites, in case you're wondering) but nothing seemed to really stick, or 'get' me. For a while, I secretly nourished the belief that the perfect to-do app for me existed. Somewhere out there. And when we meet each other, sparks will fly, it will be a match made in heaven, and someone might make a movie about it. In the end, I decided to just make it myself. While the vision might change a bit, right now it's looking like a more powerful version of Carrot -- an AI that organizes your tasks and ensures you're productive. If you'd like to be notified when this is usable, please let me know here.
